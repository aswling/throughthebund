THROUGH THE BUND is a short interactive comic about a girl who's trying to take a boat tour of the Huangpu River, but must first navigate the picturesque Bund to get to the docks. As the reader, you decide whether the character finds the tour in time, or if she gets distracted by taking too many pictures and misses the boat.
